About Us

The University of Waterloo Analytics Group for Games and Sports exists to gather students, staff, faculty, and members of the community at large to grow the presence of the quantitative analysis and applications of analytics to games and sports. Our main objectives are as follows:

  1. Facilitate collaboration on projects and teams for hackathons and competitions
  2. Share resources for tutorials and workshops to enhance our breadth of data science applications to sport
  3. Attend seminars from respected members of the sports analytics community
  4. Present research at and attend conferences

We meet in a hybrid format bi-weekly throughout the school year. Supplementary information from previous sessions can be found in the Sessions tab.
